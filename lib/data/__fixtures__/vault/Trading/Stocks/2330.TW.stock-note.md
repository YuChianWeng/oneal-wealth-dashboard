# 2330.TW — 台積電 Research

## Thesis

TSMC is the world's leading semiconductor foundry with monopolistic advantages in advanced nodes. Structural demand from AI, HPC, and 5G drives long-term revenue growth. The company's 3nm and upcoming 2nm nodes position it years ahead of competitors.

## Catalysts

- NVIDIA Blackwell GPU ramp driving 3nm demand
- Intel outsourcing more wafer production
- US CHIPS Act subsidies for Arizona fab
- Japan Kumamoto fab expansion

## Risks

- Geopolitical tension around Taiwan Strait
- Cyclical semiconductor downturn
- Power and water constraints in Taiwan
- Customer concentration (Apple ~25% of revenue)

## Invalidation

Stop-loss at 550 TWD if semiconductor cycle turns sharply negative and revenue growth goes negative for two consecutive quarters.

## Next Step

Monitor Q3 2026 earnings — focus on gross margin guidance and advanced node utilization rates.
